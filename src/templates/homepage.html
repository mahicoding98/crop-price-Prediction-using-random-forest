Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana

Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan DhanYojana (PM-KMY) on 12.9.2019 with a view to provide social security to Small and Marginal Farmers in their old age when they have no means of livelihood and minimal or no savings to take care of their expenses.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana - Crop Insurance

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana was launched on February 18, 2016, by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The objective of this scheme is to provide financial assistance to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural disasters, pests, and diseases.

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Under this scheme, farmers owning up to 2 hectares of land are eligible for benefits. Under the PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, farmers receive financial assistance in annual installments. A total of ₹6,000 per year is provided, with ₹2,000 in each installment.

Pashudhan Bima Yojana

Livestock insurance is taken to compensate for the loss caused by diseases or accidents leading to the death of animals. From this perspective, obtaining livestock insurance has become a necessity today.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchan Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) is a national initiative aimed at increasing agricultural productivity and ensuring the optimal use of various water resources across the country. The budget allocation for the next 5 years is ₹50,000 crore.
